Noodles

For 2 portions:

Ingredients:
  • 2 packs of instant noodles
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 egg
  • Veggies of your choice
Preparations:
  1. Add the noodles to about a cup of boiling water
  2. Cook for about 2 minutes then add seasoning.
  3. Boil your egg for about 10-15 minute
  4. add the veggies to the noodles and enjoy
